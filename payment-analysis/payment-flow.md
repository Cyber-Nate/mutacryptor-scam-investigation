# Payment Flow Analysis

## Step 1: Task Requirement
Victim instructed to purchase software.

## Step 2: Payment Page
- Bitcoin-only payment
- Time-limited invoice

## Step 3: Wallet Generation
- Unique wallet per transaction
- Rotating addresses

## Step 4: Transaction
Funds sent to attacker-controlled wallet.

## Step 5: No Refund
- No reimbursement processed
- No accountability

## Key Observation
Bitcoin ensures:
- Irreversibility
- Pseudonymity
- No chargeback
