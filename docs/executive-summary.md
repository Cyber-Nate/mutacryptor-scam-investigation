# Executive Summary

This investigation analyzes a coordinated scam targeting cybersecurity job seekers through fake internship offers.

The attacker creates a convincing company structure, including:
- Offer letters
- Employee portals
- Training programs
- Logistics and onboarding flows

Victims are instructed to purchase software (MutaCryptor) with the promise of reimbursement. The payment is made via Bitcoin, ensuring irreversibility and anonymity.

The infrastructure shows multiple red flags:
- Non-functional domains (NXDOMAIN)
- Rotating cryptocurrency wallets
- Lack of corporate registration

This indicates a structured financial fraud operation leveraging social engineering and fake corporate identity.

Severity: HIGH
