# Attack Chain Analysis

## Phase 1: Target Acquisition
Victims are sourced from job platforms.

## Phase 2: Initial Contact
Offer letter and onboarding email sent.

## Phase 3: Trust Building
- Employee portal access
- Manager introduction
- Structured training plan

## Phase 4: Task Assignment
Victim is asked to analyze paid software.

## Phase 5: Financial Trigger
Victim is required to purchase software.

## Phase 6: Payment Execution
Bitcoin payment required.

## Phase 7: Exit
- No reimbursement
- Domain becomes unreachable
